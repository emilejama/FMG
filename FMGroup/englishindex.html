ABOUT US
  • Since 1999, FMG has been specializing in the field of accounting, audit, tax consulting, and business development in the Baltic States. We have exceptional competencies for accounting management according to the International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS), consolidation and accounting management for large enterprises (those with over 300 employees), as well as long- term competencies in attending the accounting of trading, manufacturing, services, and virtual payment platforms.
  • We provide tax advice to natural persons and legal entities.
  • We can provide companies with registered address of actual location in Lithuania (EU) and take care of establishment and supervision of a new company.
  • High quality of FMG services is guaranteed by 36 field experts working in Vilnius, Klaipėda, Riga, Tallinn, and London.
  • We attend Lithuanian companies and international clients from the USA, Sweden, Germany, Denmark, Czech Republic, Ireland, Finland, etc.

We know we are professional at our work and we seek to prove this to our clients.

An initial investment in our customer
In the beginning of cooperation, we train our client’s personnel to complete the source accounting documents, provide with document templates, and consult on the accounting policy as well as tax planning issues without any additional consideration. For each client, whose accounting we take over from other accountants, we carry out an initial accounting and tax review, i.e. determine the quality of bookkeeping in the company.
